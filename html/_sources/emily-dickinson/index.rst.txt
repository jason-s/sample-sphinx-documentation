Emily Dickinson
===============

`Emily Dickinson <https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Emily_Dickinson>`_ (1832 - 1898) lived in Amherst, Massachusetts,
and wrote many poems that can be recited to the melody and meter of 
`The Yellow Rose of Texas <https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Yellow_Rose_of_Texas_(song)>`_.
